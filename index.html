Orca Whales of Puget Sound

Southern Resident Killer Whales

The Southern Resident Killer Whales (SRKW) are a critically endangered fish eating population of orca that frequent the waters of British Columbia, Washington, and Oregon.

Bigg's Killer Whales

Bigg's Killer Whales, previously known as Transients, are a population of mammal eating orca that frequent the waters of British Columbia, Washington, and Oregon.
